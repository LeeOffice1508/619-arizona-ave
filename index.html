The Offering

A Rare Generational
Ownership Opportunity

619 Arizona represents a premier, free-standing office property in one of Los Angeles' most desirable coastal submarkets. Meticulously designed and exceptionally maintained, the property offers a rare blend of architectural character and modern functionality.

High ceilings, abundant natural light, and thoughtful design elements give the interiors a sophistication that is refined but also versatile — accommodating everything from private executive offices to collaborative workspaces. The freestanding structure provides complete autonomy, allowing its owner to fully brand and control the environment as a true expression of company culture.

Just blocks from the core of downtown Santa Monica, yet situated on a quieter thoroughfare. This building stands out as the rare generational opportunity for a buyer seeking lasting control, long-term stability, and a distinguished presence on the Westside.
